Before You Continue....

Did you know?

Withdrawing is not the same as opting out. You must have previously chosen to register an organ donation decision in order to withdraw one.

Looking to opt-out?

If you do not want your organs donated, then you will need to record a decision not to donate your organs/tissue (opt out) on the DonorHub Organ Donor Register.

Withdraw an Existing Registration?

If you withdraw from the register, we will not have a decision about organ donation recorded for you. This means that: If you live in England, Wales or Jersey, are not in a group excluded from opt out legislation and you have not registered an organ donation decision, it will be considered that you agree to be an organ donor. This is known as deemed consent. If you withdraw and later change your mind, you may record your new organ donation decision at any time. Whatever you decide, make sure your family and loved ones know what you want.
